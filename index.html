🚀 App Features

Everything you need,
when you need it most

Calm Routines puts real-time, practical ADHD support in your pocket — designed by parents who understand.

🚨

Moment Mode

Instant in-the-moment scripts — tap what's happening and get step-by-step de-escalation guidance in seconds. No searching, just answers.

💬

AI Meltdown Support

Real-time AI chatbot that adapts to your child's age and situation, providing calming scripts and de-escalation strategies when you need them most.

📋

Daily & Weekly Planner

Visual drag-and-drop routines with point-based rewards. Build structure your child can follow with customisable task cards and milestone tracking.

🔄

Routine Builder

Visual routine builder with drag-and-drop steps, timers, and a guided playback mode children can follow independently. Build morning, bedtime, and custom routines.

📖

Calming Scripts Bank

A library of age-appropriate scripts for bedtime, transitions, meltdowns, and more — the right words at the right time, always at your fingertips.

⏱️

Focus Reset Sessions

Guided breathing exercises paired with interactive fidget tools. Parents can remote-start sessions for their child from anywhere in the house.

🎁

Rewards & Points

Children earn points for completing daily tasks. Set custom milestones and rewards to build intrinsic motivation through positive reinforcement.

📊

Meltdown Tracker

Log, track, and analyse meltdown patterns over time. Identify triggers, track improvements, and share insights with schools or professionals.

🎓

Homework Hub

Structure homework time with timers, task lists, and focus-friendly tools designed for ADHD learners. Keep sessions manageable and stress-free.

📈

Weekly Insights

AI-generated weekly reports on meltdowns, routines, and progress — viewable trends and actionable summaries you can share with schools and professionals.

🛤️ Getting Started

Up and running
in under 2 minutes

Three simple steps to bring calm and structure into your family's daily life.

1

Sign up free

Create your account and add your child's profile — their age, triggers, and what works for them. It takes less than a minute.

2

Set up routines

Use our ready-made templates or build your own daily and weekly plans — drag and drop tasks, assign points, and set rewards.

3

Get instant support

When things get tough, Moment Mode and the AI chatbot give you the right words and strategies instantly — no searching, just help.
